Zucchini Parmesan Chips

Healthy zucchini chips made with simple ingredients

Looking for a crispy snack with nutrients? Check out these parmesan zucchini chips! So good and easy to make at home.

Ingredients:

  • zucchini
  • ranch seasoning packet
  • olive oil
  • shredded parmesan

Steps:

  1. Slice zucchini into rounds. Add oil, ranch seasoning, and parmesan to your liking (we like lots of parm)
  2. Place on baking sheet and bake at 425F for 20 minutes
