Killer Crack Chicken

They call it crack chicken for a reason.

Mmmm, chicken. But you know what's better? Marianted chicken with crack sauce! Guarantee you'll cook this over and over again.

Ingredients:

  • 8 chicken thighs
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 1 tbs sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tbs worcestershire sauce
  • 1 large lemon
  • 5 tbs honey (or maple syrup)
  • 7 garlic cloves, minced
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Pat chicken dry
  2. In a large bowl, add all ingredients to chicken. Toss and coat well. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  3. Preheat oven to 450F. Transfer chicken to a baking dish and pour over the marinade. Bake about 30-35 minutes. Baste chicken half way through.
  4. If you want them browned and crispy, turn on broiler for a few minutes. Serve and enjoy!
